*Song of the Yellow Bird*:

	翩翩黃鳥，
	雌雄相依。
	念我之獨，
	誰其與歸?

English translation:
    	
	Orioles fly smoothly
	Female and male cuddle close together
	Thinking of my loneliness
	Whom shall I go with?
